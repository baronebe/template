Blog Entry 3

The Chrysler commercial for the Chrysler 200 portrays Detroit in a different way than most think of the city as. Detroit has been to hell and back and has had some very big down falls. The commercial doesn’t try to sugar coat what Detroit has been through, instead the creators chose to think of Detroit’s past as a building block that has made it a much stronger city. Because of this, the Chrysler 200 is seen to be a strong car. The song lose yourself by Eminem is included in this video due to the fact that he was born and raised in Detroit. Eminem is included to show that even though Detroit may be a not so great city currently, and even though it has had some hard falls, good can still come out of the city. Eminem is a very successful rap artist that is from Detroit. He has shown that Detroit is not a city full of failure. This commercial gives viewers a different perspective of Detroit that it is a very strong and unique city, just like the Chrysler 200. The tag line “Imported from Detroit” sends a very strong message to viewers. The definition of the word import from Merriam-Webster is to bring a product into a country to be sold. The main word that sticks out of that definition is country. Obviously, Detroit is in the United States, not in a different country, so why include the word imported? This is done to show how unique Detroit is. The US does not have any other city like Detroit. Detroit stands out, it is anything but ordinary. Detroit is like another country compared to the rest of the US. The creators included this to show how extraordinary Detroit is, along with the Chrysler 200. The main purpose of this ad is to show viewers how great of a car the Chrysler 200 is. By doing this, they compare it to Detroit, where the car is from, showing how strong and unique the car is, just like Detroit.
