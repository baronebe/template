Executive Summary

I did my formal research paper on space sciences. Space studies are one of the most intersting things to study in my opinion. Space is a scary but beautiful place, and without technology we would know not nearly as much about our universe as we do now. Technology is everything in the study of space, and the better the technology gets, the more and more we will know. The goal of space sciences is to learn more about our universe and its composition, and to ultimately learn more about the origin of life. Astronomy is a social activity in which people discuss new ideas, interpret data, and argue with each other over what certain observations mean. In astronomy there are two types of astronomers, observational and theoretical. Astronomers use two methods to display their information: research papers, and visual displays.
