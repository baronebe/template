Background Information

There are two types of astronomers, observational and theoretical.Observational astronomers specialize in building instruments such as telescopes or spacecraft.The second type of astronomer is a theoretical astronomer, also known as an astrophysicist. These people take the results of observational astronomers and attempt to provide or create physical models, which explain the data and provide details and ideas of the direction the observers should go. Astronomers have a number of different channels through which scientific results are made known. Astronomy is a lot of sitting around and doing nothing while you wait for results.
